About

I work to build a world where people age healthily and die peacefully, at a very old age, without protracted illness or disability. I pursue this through expert-centered interpretable AI methods and interactive platforms that enable more effective human-in-the-loop in-silico hypothesis testing and downstream decision-making in biomedical and clinical settings — integrating epigenomic, transcriptomic, genomic, clinical, and environmental data.

☀️ By day, I am a Senior Researcher on the Biomedical ML team at Microsoft Research Health Futures (New England Research Division) in Cambridge, MA. 🌙 By night, I serve on Boards and develop programs to empower the next generation of underrepresented groups in science.
